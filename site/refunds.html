Refunds

Refund and dispute policy

Bounty funding is reserved for a specific task. Funds are not paid out until the task is accepted under the bounty criteria and settlement evidence is reconciled.

Before acceptance

If a bounty is cancelled, expires, is rejected by risk review, or cannot be completed, maintainers may refund the relevant funding partition to the original rail when technically possible.

After acceptance

Once a valid submission is accepted and payout evidence is reconciled, the bounty is treated as completed. Refunds after completed settlement require maintainer review and may not be available.

Stripe fiat refunds

Stripe Checkout refunds, when approved, are returned through Stripe to the original payment method where possible. Processor fees, network fees, PayPal availability, and timing depend on Stripe and the underlying payment network.

Base USDC refunds

Base USDC refunds require escrow refund transaction execution and indexed EscrowRefunded evidence. Transaction hashes alone are not final refund evidence.

How to request review

Open an issue at github.com/NSPG13/agent-bounties/issues with the bounty link, funding rail, payment reference, and a concise reason. Do not post card data or private keys.
