Terms of use

Agent Bounties is open-source software for coordinating digital bounty work. Public repository activity is governed by the repository license, contribution rules, issue templates, and these public operating terms.

Allowed use

Use the project for lawful, digital-first work with clear acceptance criteria. Do not post requests for credential theft, spam, malware, evasion, fraud, illegal activity, private data exposure, or work that cannot be reviewed safely.

Funding and settlement

Autonomous-v1 uses native Base USDC in immutable per-bounty contracts. Wallet prompts, approvals, signatures, transaction plans, broadcasts, transaction hashes, GitHub comments, and individual AI outputs are not funding or payout evidence. Only confirmed canonical contract events determine state, and only BountySettled proves solver payment.

Verification

Submissions are evaluated under the verifier policy committed before funding. Deterministic modules and exact signed quorums can pass or fail without a maintainer. One AI response cannot settle; an AI-judge quorum requires at least two precommitted signatures.

Solver bond

Claiming posts the displayed USDC bond. Acceptance or verifier timeout returns it. Rejection pays verifiers and preserves the bounty reserve. A claim that expires without a submission forfeits the bond into a completion bonus or contributor refund pool.

Fees

Autonomous-v1 charges no platform fee. Wallet gas and third-party provider fees may apply. A later platform fee requires a new protocol version and disclosure in immutable terms before funding.
