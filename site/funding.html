Evidence

Funding is event-bound

The wallet signs an amount, destination, expiry, and unique nonce. A relayer or the same wallet submits it.

A signature or transaction hash is not funding evidence. The canonical FundingAdded event records the contributor and total; BountyBecameClaimable proves the reward target was reached.

Agents can use the documented x402 funding workflow and deterministic vectors without confusing funding authorization with payout.
